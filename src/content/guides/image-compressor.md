Large images can slow down your website and eat up your mobile data. Compressing images is the best way to optimize your digital presence without sacrificing visual appeal.

### Understanding Quality vs. Size
When you compress an image, you're essentially looking for the "sweet spot" where the file size is as small as possible, but the image still looks sharp to the human eye.

### How to use the Lumea Compressor
1. **Upload your image**: Works with JPG, PNG, and WebP.
2. **Adjust the Quality**: Use the slider. We recommend **80%** for a great balance of quality and size.
3. **Compare**: Check the "Reduction" percentage. You'll often see savings of 70-90%!
4. **Download**: Save your optimized image.

### Privacy First
Because our compressor runs using **Web Workers** inside your browser, you can compress sensitive photos without worrying about them being stored on a remote server. This is ideal for personal ID photos or confidential screenshots.
