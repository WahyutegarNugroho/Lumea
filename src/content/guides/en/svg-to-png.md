SVG files are great for design, but sometimes you need a raster PNG for platforms that do not support vector graphics.

### How to Convert

1. **Upload SVG**: Drop your .svg file into the upload area. You will see an instant preview.
2. **Set resolution**: Adjust the width and height, or use the scaling buttons (1x to 8x) to define output size.
3. **Convert**: Click "Convert SVG to PNG" to render the image.
4. **Download**: Your high-quality PNG will be downloaded automatically.

### Why Convert SVG to PNG?

- Universal compatibility across all platforms and apps
- Preserve vector sharpness at your chosen resolution
- Add raster effects that are not possible in SVG

### Privacy

We render your SVG directly to a high-resolution canvas. No data is sent to external servers.
