Managing the structure of a PDF document is a frequent need, whether you are compiling a report, removing irrelevant pages, or reordering sections.

### Visual Page Management

Lumea Organize tool provides a visual interface that shows high-quality thumbnails of every page in your document. You can easily see the entire document structure at a glance.

### How to Use the Organize Tool

1. **Upload your PDF**: Drag and drop your PDF file into the tool area.
2. **View thumbnails**: Each page is displayed as a preview image.
3. **Drag to reorder**: Simply drag any page thumbnail to a new position to change the order.
4. **Delete unwanted pages**: Click the remove button on any page thumbnail to delete it from the document.
5. **Save**: Click "Save Organized PDF" to download your restructured document.

### Privacy and Security

Just like all Lumea tools, the Organize feature works entirely in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your computer, ensuring your data remains private and secure at all times.
