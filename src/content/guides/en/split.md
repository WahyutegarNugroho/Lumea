Extracting pages from a large PDF is essential when you only need a portion of a document. Lumea Split makes this easy with a visual preview interface.

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Upload your PDF**: Drag and drop the file into the upload area.
2. **Browse pages**: Scroll through the page thumbnails to find the specific pages you want.
3. **Select pages**: Click on individual pages, or use the range selector (e.g., 1-5, 8, 10-12) to define which pages to extract.
4. **Split**: Click "Split PDF" to extract the selected pages into a new document.
5. **Download**: Your browser will download the new PDF containing only the extracted pages.

### No Quality Loss

Pages are extracted without re-encoding, preserving the original resolution, fonts, and layout. The resulting PDF is fully compliant with standard PDF viewers.

### Privacy

Your document is processed entirely on your device, ensuring sensitive information never leaves your computer.
