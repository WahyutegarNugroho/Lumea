Converting images to PDF is a common task for creating portfolios, digitizing receipts, or compiling photo albums. Lumea makes it simple.

### Step-by-Step

1. **Upload your images**: Drag and drop JPG, PNG, or other image files into the tool. You can add multiple images at once.
2. **Arrange the order**: The images will appear in a list. You can remove and re-add them to adjust the sequence.
3. **Convert**: Click "Convert to PDF" to combine all images into a single PDF document.
4. **Download**: Your browser will save the new PDF automatically.

### Automatic Optimization

Images are automatically sized to fit standard page dimensions. The resulting PDF is optimized for printing and digital distribution.

### Privacy

Your images are processed entirely in your browser. They are never uploaded to any server, ensuring your visual content remains private.
