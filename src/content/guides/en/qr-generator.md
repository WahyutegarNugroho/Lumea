QR codes bridge the gap between physical and digital worlds. Lumea allows you to generate them with custom colors and high resolution.

### How to Generate

1. **Enter content**: Type the URL or text you want to encode in the QR code.
2. **Customize**: Choose foreground and background colors to match your brand.
3. **Generate**: The QR code is created instantly in your browser.
4. **Download**: Save as a high-resolution PNG image.

### Best Practices

- Test your QR code before printing to ensure it scans correctly.
- Use high contrast between foreground and background colors.
- Leave adequate white space (quiet zone) around the code.

### Privacy

Your data never leaves your device. The QR code is generated entirely in your browser, keeping your URLs and text 100% private.
