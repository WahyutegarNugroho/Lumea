Have you ever opened a PDF only to find that some pages are rotated incorrectly? The Lumea Rotate tool lets you fix this permanently.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your PDF**: Select the file you want to fix.
2. **Choose rotation**: Click "90° Left" or "90° Right" to rotate all pages in the document.
3. **Preview**: The tool processes the rotation and updates the document.
4. **Download**: Click "Save & Download PDF" to get your corrected file.

### Permanent Fix

The rotation is applied directly to the PDF's internal page metadata. This means the orientation stays fixed no matter which PDF viewer or device you use to open the file.

### Privacy First

Your document is processed entirely within your browser. The file never leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy.
