Different use cases call for different image formats. Lumea Format Converter lets you switch between JPG, PNG, and WebP instantly.

### Which Format Should You Choose?

- **WebP**: The best choice for modern websites. It offers superior compression and supports transparency.
- **PNG**: Use this for logos, icons, and graphics that require perfect clarity and transparency support.
- **JPG**: The standard for photographs where a small file size is more important than perfect detail.

### How to Convert

1. **Upload your image**: Select any supported image file.
2. **Choose output format**: Select JPG, PNG, or WebP.
3. **Convert**: The tool processes your image instantly.
4. **Download**: Save your converted image.

### Privacy

All conversions are performed locally in your browser. Your images never leave your computer.
