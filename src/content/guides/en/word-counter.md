Accurate text statistics are essential for writers, students, and professionals who need to meet specific length requirements.

### Features

- **Word count**: Know exactly how many words your text contains.
- **Character count**: Count with and without spaces.
- **Sentence count**: Understand your text structure.
- **Reading time**: Estimated based on an average reading speed of 225 words per minute.

### How to Use

1. **Paste your text**: Type or paste content into the input area.
2. **Instant results**: All statistics update automatically as you type.
3. **Copy or clear**: Use the copy button to save your work or clear to start over.

### Privacy

Your text never leaves your browser. Lumea processes everything locally, making it safe for sensitive content like manuscripts or confidential reports.
