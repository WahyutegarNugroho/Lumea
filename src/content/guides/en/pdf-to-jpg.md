There are many reasons to convert PDF pages to images: sharing on social media, embedding in presentations, or archiving visual content.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your PDF**: Select the document you want to convert.
2. **High-quality rendering**: The tool uses a 2.0x scale factor to ensure exported images are crisp and legible.
3. **Download**: Each page will be downloaded individually as a separate JPG image file.

### Use Cases

- Extract charts and graphics from reports
- Share contract pages on messaging apps
- Create preview thumbnails for portfolios
- Archive visual content from PDF documents

### Privacy

All conversion happens locally. Your PDF data never leaves your computer, and no copies are stored on any server.
