Digitize your physical documents, screenshots, and photos instantly with Lumea's private, on-device OCR scanner.

### How It Works

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) analyzes images to detect and extract text characters. Lumea uses Tesseract.js, a powerful OCR engine that runs entirely in your browser.

### Step-by-Step

1. **Upload image**: Drop your image (JPG, PNG, WebP) into the upload area. For best results, ensure the text is clear and well-lit.
2. **Select language**: Choose the primary language of the text. This helps the AI recognize characters more accurately.
3. **Scan**: Click "Extract Text". The OCR engine processes the image.
4. **Copy**: Your editable text appears in the result box, ready to use.

### Tips for Best Results

- Use high-resolution images with good lighting
- Ensure text is not skewed or rotated
- Select the correct language for better accuracy

### Privacy

All OCR processing happens on your device. Your documents and extracted text never leave your computer.
