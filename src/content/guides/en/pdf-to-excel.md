Extracting data from PDF tables into Excel is a common need for financial analysis, data entry, and research. Lumea makes this process seamless.

### How to Convert

1. **Upload your PDF**: Select the document containing tables.
2. **Smart detection**: The tool automatically identifies rows and columns to reconstruct your tables.
3. **Download**: Get your data as an editable .xlsx file compatible with Excel and Google Sheets.

### Best Use Cases

- Extracting financial reports and balance sheets
- Converting survey results from PDF to analyzable data
- Digitizing printed tables and forms

### Privacy

All extraction happens locally. We never see your data, and your financial records remain exclusively on your device.
