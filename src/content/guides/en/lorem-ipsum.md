Lorem ipsum is the industry standard for placeholder text. It mimics the natural distribution of letters and words in English, making it perfect for layout testing.

### Generation Options

- **Paragraphs**: Best for filling large content areas.
- **Sentences**: Ideal for short descriptions or feature lists.
- **Words**: Perfect for labels, buttons, or titles.

### How to Use

1. Choose the type of output you need (paragraphs, sentences, or words).
2. Set the amount you want to generate.
3. Click "Generate" and the placeholder text appears instantly.
4. Copy the result for use in your designs.

### Privacy

All generation is done locally. Lumea does not track or store any generated content.
