Barcodes are essential for retail, inventory management, and logistics. Lumea lets you generate them instantly without any tracking.

### How to Use

1. **Select format**: Choose from Code 128 (general purpose), EAN-13 (retail), or UPC-A (North American retail).
2. **Input data**: Enter the text or numeric code you want to encode.
3. **Preview**: The barcode updates instantly as you type.
4. **Download**: Save as a high-resolution PNG ready for printing.

### Tips

- Validate your barcode data according to the format specifications.
- For retail use, ensure your EAN or UPC codes are registered and unique.
- Download at high resolution for best print quality.

### Privacy

Your data is never sent to any server. We generate all barcodes locally using high-fidelity rendering.
