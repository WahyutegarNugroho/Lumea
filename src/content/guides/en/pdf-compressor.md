Large PDF files can be difficult to email, slow to upload, and wasteful of storage space. Compression solves these problems.

### How Compression Works

PDF compression works by optimizing embedded images, removing redundant data, and streamlining the document structure. Lumea applies these techniques intelligently to maximize size reduction while preserving readability.

### Using the Compressor

1. **Upload your PDF**: Select the document you want to compress.
2. **Choose compression level**: Select from available options depending on your need for quality versus file size.
3. **Compress**: Click the compress button to process your file.
4. **Compare**: See the reduction percentage to understand how much space you saved.
5. **Download**: Save the optimized PDF to your device.

### Best Practices

- For documents with many images, expect 50-80% size reduction.
- For text-only PDFs, compression is typically less dramatic but still helpful.
- Always review the compressed output to ensure quality meets your standards.

### Privacy Guarantee

Your documents are processed locally and never transmitted over the network. This makes Lumea ideal for sensitive business documents.
