Adding a watermark to your PDF is an effective way to protect your intellectual property, mark documents as confidential, or add branding.

### Customization Options

1. **Custom Text**: Enter any text you want as your watermark, such as "Confidential", "Draft", or your company name.
2. **Font Size**: Adjust the size of the watermark text to suit your document.
3. **Opacity**: Control the transparency level so the watermark does not obscure the content.
4. **Rotation**: Choose between horizontal, vertical, or diagonal placement.
5. **Color**: Select a color that matches your brand or security requirements.

### How to Apply

1. Upload your PDF file.
2. Enter your watermark text and adjust the settings.
3. Click "Apply Watermark & Download".
4. Your browser will download the watermarked PDF instantly.

### Privacy Assurance

All watermarking happens locally in your browser. Your original and watermarked documents never leave your device.
