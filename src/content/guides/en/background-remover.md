Removing backgrounds from photos is essential for product photography, profile pictures, and creative design. Lumea uses advanced AI to do this entirely in your browser.

### How It Works

Lumea uses the MediaPipe Selfie Segmentation model to identify the subject in your photo. The AI runs on your device's GPU or CPU, not on a remote server.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your image**: Drop a photo (JPG, PNG, WebP) into the tool.
2. **AI processing**: The model detects the subject and separates it from the background.
3. **Refine**: Adjust edge smoothing and refinement intensity for perfect results.
4. **Download**: Save your image as a transparent PNG.
