Cropping is one of the most common photo editing tasks. Whether you are preparing images for social media, printing, or web publishing, Lumea makes it easy.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your image**: Select the photo you want to crop.
2. **Adjust the crop area**: Drag the handles to select the portion you want to keep.
3. **Choose a preset**: Use standard ratios like 1:1 for profile pictures, 4:3 for presentations, or 16:9 for videos.
4. **Crop & download**: Save your cropped image instantly.

### Pro Tips

- Use the 1:1 ratio for perfect profile pictures.
- For printing, maintain the original aspect ratio to avoid stretching.
- Crop first, then resize for optimal results.

### Privacy

All cropping happens locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device.
