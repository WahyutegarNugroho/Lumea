Converting PDFs to editable Word documents is often necessary when you need to modify the content of a PDF or reuse its text in other documents.

### How to Convert

1. **Upload your PDF**: Select the PDF file you wish to convert.
2. **Processing**: Lumea extracts text content and reconstructs the document structure.
3. **Download**: Your browser downloads the resulting .docx file.

### What to Expect

- Text content becomes fully editable in Microsoft Word or Google Docs.
- Simple layouts and formatting are preserved.
- Complex layouts with tables, columns, or unusual fonts may require manual adjustments after conversion.

### Privacy

Your document is processed entirely on your device. No files are uploaded to any server, keeping your content secure.
