Vector images are mathematically defined, meaning they can be scaled to any size without becoming pixelated. This is essential for logos, icons, and illustrations.

### Why Vectorize?

- **Infinite scaling**: SVGs look sharp at any size, from favicons to billboards.
- **Small file size**: Vector paths are more efficient than pixel data.
- **Editability**: SVGs can be edited in any vector design software.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your image**: Choose a raster image (JPG, PNG) to convert.
2. **Select a preset**: Choose from "Default", "Logo / Flat" for sharp edges, "High Detail" for complex images, or "Black & White".
3. **Vectorize**: Click to process the image and generate SVG paths.
4. **Download**: Save the resulting SVG file.

### Privacy

Your images are processed entirely in your browser. No data is sent to external servers, ensuring your designs remain private.
