Merging PDF files is a common task for students, researchers, and business professionals. Whether you're combining several reports into a single document or organizing scanned receipts, doing it securely is paramount.

### Why Lumea is Different
Most "online PDF mergers" require you to upload your sensitive documents to their cloud servers. This poses a significant privacy risk. Lumea's Merge tool works entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

### Step-by-Step Guide
1. **Prepare your files**: Ensure all the PDF documents you want to merge are accessible on your device.
2. **Upload to Lumea**: Drag and drop your files into the Merge PDF tool area.
3. **Check the order**: Lumea allows you to see the list of files. Currently, you can remove and re-add them to get the perfect sequence.
4. **Click Merge**: Hit the "Merge PDFs" button.
5. **Download**: Your browser will instantly generate and download the combined document.

### Technical Details
We use the industry-standard `pdf-lib` to handle the low-level PDF manipulation. This ensures that the generated document is compliant with the PDF 1.7 specification and remains readable in all modern viewers.
