Resizing images is a fundamental task for web developers, social media managers, and anyone who needs images at specific dimensions.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your image**: Select any image file.
2. **Set dimensions**: Enter the desired width and height in pixels.
3. **Lock aspect ratio**: Keep proportions intact automatically by enabling the lock.
4. **Resize**: Click "Resize & Download" to process your image.
5. **Save**: Download the resized image.

### Tips

- For social media, use recommended dimensions (e.g., 1200x630 for link sharing).
- Locking the aspect ratio prevents distortion.
- Use "Image Compressor" after resizing to further optimize file size.

### Privacy

Your images are processed using canvas technology with high-quality interpolation. Everything stays on your device.
