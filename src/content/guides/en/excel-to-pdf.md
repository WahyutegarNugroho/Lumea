Converting spreadsheets to PDF is ideal for sharing reports, financial statements, and data summaries with stakeholders who do not need editing access.

### How to Use

1. **Upload your Excel file**: Select your .xlsx or .xls file.
2. **Automatic conversion**: The spreadsheet data is formatted into clean, readable tables in the PDF.
3. **Download**: Save the resulting PDF document.

### Key Features

- Multi-sheet support for converting workbook contents
- Clean table rendering for professional appearance
- Preserved formatting for numbers, dates, and text

### Privacy

Your financial and business data is handled with maximum security. Everything stays in your browser, and no data is sent to any server.
