Formatting text properly is important for writing, coding, and content creation. Lumea Case Converter makes it effortless.

### Available Cases

- **UPPERCASE**: Converts every letter to capital letters.
- **lowercase**: Converts every letter to small letters.
- **Title Case**: Capitalizes The First Letter Of Each Word.
- **Sentence case**: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence.
- **Capitalize**: Capitalizes the first letter of each word.

### How to Use

1. Paste or type your text into the input area.
2. Click the desired case option.
3. The converted text appears instantly, ready to copy.

### Privacy

All text processing is done locally in your browser. Your content never leaves your device.
